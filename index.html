Who is Kim Bok Ju?

Kim Bok-Ju is a Korean actor Lee Sung-kyung from the Korean drama 'Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-ju' in 2016.

If you watch this drama, you will have no choice but to be a fan of Lee Sung-kyung's cute side. So do I.
